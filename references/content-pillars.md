|--------|---------|------------|

## How to Choose Pillars

1. **Start with 4-5 pillars** — enough variety without spreading too thin
2. **Balance value vs. promotion** — aim for 80% value / 20% promotional
3. **Match to platform strengths:**
   - Instagram → Visual pillars (Product, Lifestyle, Behind-the-Scenes)
   - LinkedIn → Authority pillars (Thought Leadership, Case Studies, Tips)
   - Twitter/X → Conversational pillars (Opinions, Tips, Memes)
   - TikTok → Entertaining pillars (Behind-the-Scenes, Tutorials, Relatable)
4. **Test and refine** — drop pillars that don't resonate, double down on what works
