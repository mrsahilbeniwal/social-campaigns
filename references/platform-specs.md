# Social Media Platform Specifications

Quick reference for image dimensions, character limits, and posting best practices per platform, keep this in mind while generating any image.

---

## Instagram

### Image Dimensions
| Format | Dimensions | Aspect Ratio |
|--------|-----------|--------------|
| Square Post | 1080 × 1080 px | 1:1 |
| Portrait Post | 1080 × 1350 px | 4:5 (recommended) |
| Landscape Post | 1080 × 566 px | 1.91:1 |
| Story / Reel | 1080 × 1920 px | 9:16 |
| Carousel (per slide) | 1080 × 1080 px or 1080 × 1350 px | 1:1 or 4:5 |

### Caption Limits
- **Max length:** 2,200 characters
- **Ideal length:** 125-150 characters (before "more" truncation)
- **Hashtags:** 5-15 per post (mix of popular + niche)

### Best Practices
- First line of caption is the hook — make it count
- Use line breaks for readability (no wall of text)
- Carousel posts get 1.4x more reach than single images
- Reels get priority in the algorithm
- Post between 11AM-1PM or 7PM-9PM local time for most audiences

## LinkedIn

### Image Dimensions
| Format | Dimensions | Aspect Ratio |
|--------|-----------|--------------|
| Single Image | 1200 × 627 px | 1.91:1 |
| Square Image | 1080 × 1080 px | 1:1 |
| Portrait Image | 1080 × 1350 px | 4:5 |
| Carousel (PDF) | 1080 × 1080 px or 1080 × 1350 px per page | 1:1 or 4:5 |

### Caption Limits
- **Max length:** 3,000 characters
- **Ideal length:** 150-300 characters (before "see more" fold)
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 per post (professional and industry-relevant)

### Best Practices
- First 2 lines must hook — LinkedIn truncates aggressively
- Document/PDF carousels get massive organic reach
- Personal stories outperform corporate announcements
- Post Tuesday-Thursday, 8AM-10AM or 12PM for B2B
- Engage in comments within first hour for algorithm boost

## Twitter / X

### Image Dimensions
| Format | Dimensions | Aspect Ratio |
|--------|-----------|--------------|
| Single Image | 1200 × 675 px | 16:9 |
| Two Images | 700 × 800 px each | 7:8 |
| Four Images | 700 × 800 px each | 7:8 |

### Caption Limits
- **Max length:** 280 characters (free) / 25,000 characters (premium)
- **Ideal length:** 71-100 characters for engagement
- **Hashtags:** 1-3 max (more looks spammy)

### Best Practices
- Threads outperform single tweets for thought leadership
- Images increase engagement by 150%
- Quote tweets with commentary build authority
- Post 8AM-10AM and 6PM-9PM for most audiences
- Engage with replies — conversation drives impressions

## General Rules Across All Platforms

1. **Safe zones:** Keep critical text/elements away from edges — at least 100px from all sides
2. **Text on images:** Maximum 20% of image area should be text (Facebook/Instagram penalize text-heavy images)
3. **File formats:** PNG for graphics with text, JPG/WebP for photography
4. **File size:** Keep under 5MB for fast loading
5. **Accessibility:** Use high contrast for text overlays, describe images in alt text
6. **Branding:** Logo should be subtle — small corner placement, not centered
